About Henny

Henny is an 8 year old pure bred chihuahua. I met Henny when he was about 1 year old, around the time when my wife and I started dating. He's not the typical chihuahua that barks at the wall for no reason, he's quite the opposite. He will lay quietly all day watching your every move and taking any chance he can get to jump in your lap and cuddle the day away. The only time he makes a peep is when the doorbell rings or when you take too long to get his food into his bowl.
