From Hate to Love

When we first brough Zay home it seemed like Henny would never accept him as a brother. Zay, being an 8 week old puppy, would try to play with Henny all day long and Henny would either run away or bark and snarl at him. But over time Henny started to accept Zay and seems to be protective of him. When I take Zay outside Henny will sit and wait by the door until we come back and greet him by playfully chasing him down the hallway into the living room. Now the two are inseparable and what i would call two pups in a pod.
